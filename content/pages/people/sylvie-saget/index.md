Sylvie Saget is a PhD/Doctoral Researcher in Computational Linguistics. Her research focuses on conversational agents that can talk and act autonomously such as virtual humans, robots - or, generally speaking, any kind of object or system involving social interaction.

Her research focuses on the modeling of reasoning and dialogue via a transdisciplinary approach mixing philosophy of mind, logic and psychology. Application areas she is focusing on:
-Interaction management technologies
-Conversational / intelligent agent architectures
-Responsible conception and design methods
-Social agents as tools in psychology
